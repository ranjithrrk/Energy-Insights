About Me

I am an energy professional with over 15 years of experience across India’s natural gas and LNG value chain.

I currently work at IndianOil Corporation Limited, where my role has involved managing long-term LNG contracts, participating in equity LNG investments, and navigating complex commercial negotiations during periods of global price volatility.

Over the years, I have been closely associated with initiatives that helped cushion Indian gas consumers during high-price cycles through contract renegotiations and portfolio recalibration. My current focus is on gas marketing and demand creation, particularly in aligning LNG with India’s evolving energy needs.

Energy Insights is my attempt to create a platform to share independent, data-driven perspectives on India’s energy markets, drawing from market signals, policy developments, and commercial realities. The coverage spans:

  • LNG and natural gas markets
  • Power price behaviour and fuel-switching economics
  • CNG/CGD, fertiliser, and mobility demand drivers
  • Infrastructure-led demand creation
  • Hydrogen, renewables, and transition pathways
  • Policy and regulatory inflection points shaping India’s energy future

The views expressed are personal and analytical, intended to support informed discussion and better decision-making in a rapidly evolving energy ecosystem. They do not represent the views of my company or any of its affiliates.
